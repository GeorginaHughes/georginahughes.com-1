  • London Bridge Media

    August 2013 - October 2015

    When I first started at London Bridge Media they were in the process of rapid expansion and a shift of focus within the business. During my time here I helped to introduce Scrum processes and practices, which included configuring TFS for project management in line with the adopted processes. As a more technical and more senior member of the team I was responsible for managing the development environments of the team, mentoring junior members of team, and managing contractors when we had them.

    The Scrum process that the team was using when I started was not being adhered to correctly, and the wider company did not have an understanding of the new requirements that Scrum had for them. The team and I underwent training to become Certified Scrum Masters. This allowed the team a formal understanding of the responsibilities of the different team roles. Over the course of the next six months I facilitated the team's adoption of TFS' built in project management tools until we moved over to Jira.

    I facilitated my team's creation of a Scrum board that met our needs, and allowed the administration side of the business visibility of our daily progress. We used both virtual and physical boards as this allowed maximum visibility throughout the business. During my time on this team, we introduced many new ceremonies such as backlog grooming, sprint planning, and retrospectives. As we introduced these practices we saw great improvements in the team and we used this to motivate not only the team but the wider business to increase the amount of agile integration. By the time I left the organisation the team's clients within the business were beginning to write requirement tickets for the team using Given, When, Then scenarios.

    In the technical part of my role, shortly after I started we decided to rebuild our primary product - Betway.com - using AngularJs. This was a new technology to everyone within the company and I took the lead for the creation of the AngularJs application. Throughout my time here I took it upon myself to research new technologies to improve the development process.

    I helped my team transition from using TFS source control to Git. I helped design the new workflow that would be required as part of the change from a centralised system to a distributed system.
